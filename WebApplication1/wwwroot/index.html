01 — About

A Little About Me

Hey, I'm Edward — a 36-year-old full stack software engineer based in Virginia Beach. I genuinely love life, and I try to bring an open mind and good energy into everything. I recently completed the intensive TrueCoders Academy Full Stack Software Engineering bootcamp, where I built real-world applications from the ground up using C#, ASP.NET Core, SQL, JavaScript, and more.

My background is anything but conventional. For over ten years I've worked as an educator, helping people grow — a skill that translates directly into collaborative development environments. At the same time, I've run my own creative business customizing shoes for clients, and I do tattoo work as well. My final project at TrueCoders — Ink Your Shoes — is a live commission platform I built and deployed, combining all of that: an intake system for custom shoe and tattoo orders.

Working with me, you will get creativity, discipline, and a genuine passion for problem-solving. I'm actively looking for a team where I can keep growing and contribute from day one.
